## How AI Helped
AI was used to clarify concepts around Java concurrency that were covered in lecture, specifically around AtomicInteger and the java.util.concurrent package. It helped confirm my understanding of why synchronizing only part of a critical section is insufficient. The analysis of the code, identification of shared resources, and evaluation of each fix were done independently based on class notes and lecture material.

## Reflection
Using AI as a reference tool helped me verify my reasoning rather than replace it. I found it most useful for double-checking technical details rather than generating answers, which helped me better understand the material.
